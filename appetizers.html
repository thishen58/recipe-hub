Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Place baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast in the oven for 5-7 minutes until golden.
  4. Top each toasted slice with the tomato mixture.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic and chopped mushroom stems until soft.
  4. Mix sautéed mixture with breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stuff each mushroom cap with the mixture.
  6. Place mushrooms on a baking tray and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  7. Serve warm.
